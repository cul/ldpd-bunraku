The Gion Festival

(祇園祭礼信仰記) (Gion sairei shinkōki)

First Staged: 1757
Reference: Kokuritsu Gekijō jōen shiryōshū, edited by Kokuritsu Gekijō Geinō Chōsashitsu, vol. 203, May 1982; Hironaga, Shūzaburō, The Bunraku Handbook (Tokyo: Maison des Arts, 1976); Bunraku May 1982 Performance Program
