The Twins and the Sumida River

(雙生隅田川) (Futago Sumidagawa)

First Staged: 1720
Reference: Hironaga, Shūzaburō, The Bunraku Handbook (Tokyo: Maison des Arts, 1976); Chikamatsu: 5 Late Plays, translated and annotated by C. Andrew Gerstle (New York: Columbia University Press, c2001); February 1988 Program

Written By: Chikamatsu Monzaemon (近松門左衛門)

Performances:

  • September 1973: The Twins and the Sumida River
  • October 1973: The Twins and the Sumida River
  • February 1988: The Twins and the Sumida River
  • February 1997: The Twins and the Sumida River

Kashira:

  • Fukeoyama (老女形)
  • Kani (かに)
  • Otoko koyaku (男子役)
  • Onna koyaku (女子役)
  • Hagataki (端敵)
  • Kōmei (孔明)
  • Ōdanshichi (大団七)
  • Bunshichi (文七)
  • Otoko chū koyaku (男中子役)

Characters:

  • Sota, in fact Shichiro (猿嶋惣太実は淡路七郎)
  • Karaito (惣太女房から糸)
  • Sajitayu (蠅頭の左次太夫)
  • Bozuko (坊主子)
  • Mutsu (六)
  • Suika (すいか(すいくわ))
  • Yazo (はねわなの弥蔵(弥次))
  • Umewakamaru (吉田梅若丸)
  • Takekuni (県の権正武国)
  • Hanjogozen (班女御前)
  • Hokaibo, in fact Tengu (山伏法界坊実は天狗)
  • Matsuwakamaru (吉田松若丸)
