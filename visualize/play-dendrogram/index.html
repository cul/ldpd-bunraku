Dendrogram of a play

Below is an interactive, hierarchical graph of the structure of one play: Oshun and Dembei (Chikagoro kawara no tatehiki / 近頃河原の達引), including links to its authors and productions in which it was performed, which in turn link to scenes which link to performer information.

You can click on the circles to open or collapse nodes or double-click on one with a bolded name to learn more about its corresponding author, performance, scene, performer, character, or kashira (puppet).
